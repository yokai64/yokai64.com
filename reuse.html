Video and text Content Re-use policy

My personal stance on fair use and

how I allow my content to be re-used

Not allowed

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, I do not give permission or allow for my:

  • voice, face or any other part of me to be reconstructed, modified, manipulated or otherwise be used by AI or used in AI models.

I believe that AI is an affront to creative works and the human spirit. AI can be a tool for productivity, however I completely disagree with how it is currently being deployed - being used to replace animators, artists and even people not in those types of creative spaces such as software engineers etc.

  • content to be used in a so-called "reaction" context.

As always, "Fair Use" clauses such as actual commentary is allowed, but just leaving videos playing during streams and going AFK or saying nothing except for the odd interjection is not ok. You know the types, and you know who you are.

  • content to be downloaded for the purposes of re-distribution.

You may not download my works in order to reupload to other websites or host on other platforms, or re-distribute in any way.

Permitted

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, I allow everyone to:

  • download my content for the purposes of personal or small group offline viewing

I understand that there are circumstances in which you have limited or no access to internet connectivity for a period of time, and you may want to watch my videos or other content but won't be able to. I am humbled that you want to watch my stuff to the point where you cache it to watch at a later time! Additionally, if you want to download to watch with friends or in a classroom (for example) I permit this.

  • show or re-use clips of my content in "reaction" content for the purposes of valid criticism.

Yes, this is allowed as part of "Fair Use", if I understand it correctly. I understand that I'll probably get something wrong at some point. I would prefer if you reached out first before doing a public take-down, but I can't stop you if you decide to. I'm always open to feedback, so please take the private route before going scorched earth!

  • show or re-use clips from my content to reiterate a point in a video not solely related to my work.

Again - this is probably allowed by fair use, but if you wanna use clips from my videos to make a point or even as a joke go ahead.

  • play my content on a stream - so long as it's not a straight rip.

You may play my videos on a stream if you wish, however: you must not sit there and say nothing (or otherwise add nothing to the content) for extended periods of time - see the "Not Allowed" section above.

If you see anybody breaking this policy, please get in touch - you can use Discord, Twitter or TikTok. Preferably, use Discord!

This does not extend to other creative works, such as music or code. For those, please see the license for the respective release of those works.
